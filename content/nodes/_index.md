Our network is a mix of **personal nodes** (carried by community members) and **fixed repeaters** (solar-powered, mounted on rooftops and towers) that provide always-on coverage.

## Repeater Infrastructure

Fixed repeaters are the backbone of the mesh — always-on nodes that relay messages between areas of the city. Current repeater locations:

## Coverage

Coverage depends on terrain, buildings, and where community members are located with their personal nodes. Generally:

- **Inner Loop** — solid coverage, multiple overlapping repeaters
- **Inner suburbs** — partial, growing
- **Outer suburbs** — sparse, your node helps!

An interactive map powered by community-reported node data is in development. In the meantime, join our **Discord** for the most current coverage info.
