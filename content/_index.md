## What Is HoustonMesh?

HoustonMesh is a **free, community-built radio network** covering Houston using [Meshtastic](https://meshtastic.org) and [MeshCore](https://meshcore.co.uk) — open-source software running on affordable LoRa radio hardware.

Think of it as a **city-wide group text** that works completely off-grid — no internet, no cell towers, no subscriptions, no servers owned by any company. Just radios talking to radios, hopping messages across Houston.

---

## How Does It Work?

Imagine passing a note in class. You write your message, make a few copies, and hand them to the people nearest you. They do the same — passing copies along until everyone in the room has it. That's mesh networking.

In our case, the "notes" are digital packets sent over **900 MHz LoRa radio**. Each Meshtastic device is both a sender and a repeater — your message hops automatically from node to node until it reaches its destination, even if individual nodes are miles apart.

**No internet. No cell service. No single point of failure.**

---

## Why LoRa?

LoRa (Long Range) radio can send small messages **miles** on a single charge, through buildings, across neighborhoods, and even city-wide when paired with elevated repeater nodes. It's the same band used by IoT sensors worldwide — unlicensed, free to use, and purpose-built for low-power, long-range communication.

Our repeaters run **24/7 on solar power**, mounted on rooftops and towers across Houston.

---

## What Can You Do With It?

- **Send text messages** to the whole mesh or direct to a contact
- **Share your GPS location** with friends and family offline
- **Check in during emergencies** when cell networks are overloaded or down
- **Monitor weather stations** and sensors connected to the mesh
- **Meet your neighbors** — Houston has a growing community of mesh enthusiasts

---

## Meshtastic vs. MeshCore

We support both platforms. **Meshtastic** is the most popular entry point — it works with dozens of off-the-shelf devices and pairs to your phone via Bluetooth. **MeshCore** is a newer, more advanced alternative with different routing capabilities. [Learn more →](/learn/)
